What We Do

We provide free after-school tutoring to elementary school students to help prepare for MCAP, the Maryland state math assessment. Our tutoring combines math games and activities with a comprehensive curriculum to motivate the students and enhance their mathematical thinking abilities.

Our weekly tutoring session runs from 3:45 to 4:45 pm at Baltimore International Academy West. Our program does not run for the days when BIA West is closed. Contact us for information on how to join!


Math Literacy Nights

We outreach to parents and prospective students through fun math activities that spark an interest for math. The goal of our math night is to attract more students across the Baltimore City Area. Feel free to contact us for information on the next math night.

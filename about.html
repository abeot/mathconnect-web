Our Tutors

Albert Cao

Founder, President

Albert has had extensive experience with math teaching ever since joining his middle school math club in sixth grade. He has previously tutored elementary schoolers with mathematics and programming. Albert also has been competing in math contests for over six years, and recently qualified for the prestigious USA Junior Math Olympiad, placing him as the top fifty mathletes in the nation. Aside from math, Albert also fences competitively at the national level, and likes to listen to Beach House.

Martin Meister

Co-Founder, Alum President

Martin has always been passionate for learning math and science, and now wants to give back to his community and help others learn math. He has been tutoring math to elementary and middle school students for five years, including competitive math from middle schoolers to helping second through fourth graders. Martin has also been competing in math competitions for seven years, recently qualifying for the American Invitational Math Exam. His favorite subjects are physics and engineering, and his hobbies outside of school include playing trombone, running, playing frisbee, and folding origami.
